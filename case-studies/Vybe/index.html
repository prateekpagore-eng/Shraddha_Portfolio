Vybe OTT App

App Design • 2025

Vybe is a modern OTT streaming platform designed to deliver a seamless and immersive entertainment experience for today's digital-first users. With a growing library of movies, series, and exclusive content, the platform focuses on effortless content discovery, personalized viewing journeys, and a visually engaging interface. This project explores how strategic UX thinking and refined UI design can elevate engagement balancing discoverability, performance, and delight across the entire viewing experience.

OTT Platform . Engagement . Product Design . Binge Behaviour

The Challenge

Most OTT platforms today focus heavily on content quantity and standard navigation patterns, overlooking emotional UX, personalization, and mood-based discovery. Users often feel overwhelmed, disconnected, or uninspired while browsing struggling to find content that truly matches their current vibe or viewing intent.

The Approach

User Research

Before designing VYBE, it was essential to understand how users interact with current OTT platforms, what frustrates them, what they enjoy, and what they wish these platforms offered. Simultaneously, reviewing market trends helped identify key gaps and innovation opportunities.

Research Goals

Understand how users currently discover content on OTT apps. Identify popular platforms preferences (Netflix, Hotstar, Prime Video, etc.) Explore emotional and mood-based usage patterns. Spot opportunities in personalization.

Methods Used

Quick user surveys and informal interviews (5 OTT users) Secondary research through articles & user reviews.

Target Users

Age: 18–45 Devices: Primarily mobile, some tablet use Behavior: Binge-watchers, casual scrollers, movie lovers, multi-taskers Usage Times: Evenings, weekends, commutes

User Pain Points

Content Overload Annoying Autoplay Inconsistent Cross-Device Sync Cluttered watchlist Confusing Navigation Repetitive Recommendations Subtitle/Audio Customization Lack of Social Integration

The Solution

VYBE reimagines the OTT experience as an emotion-first, vibe-driven platform where content is discovered not just by category, but by how it makes the user feel.

With a mobile-first, mood design approach and immersive features like 'My VYBE Music,' VYBE lets users explore, feel, and engage with content in a way that reflects their unique state of mind.

Every screen, interaction and feature is crafted to 'feel' right — not just look good.
